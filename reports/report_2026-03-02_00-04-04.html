5. Conclusiones y Recomendaciones

Key Insights

  • 📉 Lost Traction: We lost significant ground (-35% relative share) in the last week despite a growing market.
  • 🛡️ Competitive Gap: Nutrition Geeks is winning on organic conversion, not just price.
  • 🏷️ Promo Inefficiency: Deals are not yielding expected uplift; our deal-ASINs are underperforming non-deal competitors.
  • 📍 Rank Disconnect: We have decent visibility (rank), but low click-through (share), indicating a listing content or social proof issue.
  • ⚠️ Risk: Continued share erosion could lead to organic rank degradation in coming weeks.

Actionable Recommendations

  • ✅ Audit Main Image: Low CTR despite visibility suggests our main image or title is less appealing than Nutrition Geeks. A/B test immediately.
  • ✅ Review Pricing Strategy: Since deals aren't working, consider a stable everyday lower price or increasing coupon visibility instead of "Deal" badges which seem ignored.
  • ✅ Focus on 'Unknown' Keywords: The "Unknown Brand" segment has high share; investigate long-tail keywords where we might be missing out.

6. Other Strategic Insights

An anomaly was detected in the "Unknown Brand" segment (mostly generic searches or smaller players), which holds a steady 10-12% of the market. This fragmentation suggests there is still room for a strong brand to consolidate the "long tail" of the market if they can improve organic ranking on secondary keywords.
